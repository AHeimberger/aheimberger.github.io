Student Project: Visionary Drive

From 2011 to 2012 I was taking part in a students project called Visionary Drive. I worked with some motivated guys on creating a wireless controlled RC-Car. Together with Daniel Hölzl I was in charge of the client, server application and the video stream. You can get more information on the project website.

Responsible for:

  • Design of the Poster
  • Design of the Website with Drupal
  • Design of the Car with Solid Works
  • Research Linux Kernel/SPI
  • Client Software Development in Java
